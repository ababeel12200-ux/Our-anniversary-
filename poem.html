My Poem for Youuu ❤️

Even miles apart, I feel you near, Your laughter, your smile, your voice so clear. 1.6 years of love, of dreams we share, Every moment with you is beyond compare.

Though oceans divide us, and cities keep us apart, You are always the home I carry in my heart. Random calls in the middle of the night, Video chats that make everything feel right.

Your messages linger, your words feel warm, In this crazy world, you are my calm, my charm. Every little memory, every tiny delight, Keeps me holding on till I see you in sight.

Shaima, my love, my joy, my pride, Through every distance, you are by my side. 1.6 years have flown, yet it feels brand new, Every day I’m thankful, my heart chose you.

No distance can break this bond so true, My world, my heart, my everything is you. So here I am, with all my love to give, Will you stay with me forever, and make this love truly live?
